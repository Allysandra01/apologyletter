Sending a Letter

A Sorry Letter Messenger makes it easy to repair misunderstandings through the time-honored gesture of letter writing. Here's how to craft and send yours:

1

Draft Your Apology

Fill out the Subject line and start writing. As your words flow, add as many parchment pages as you need to capture your thoughts fully.

2

Choose a Surprise Seal

Choose an icon (Love, Peace, Comfort, etc.). This icon will seal your virtual envelope and burst out dynamically when opened.

3

Generate Your Letter Link

Click "Send & Create Link". The system translates your text, pages, and surprise choice into a secure, shortened, compressed URL parameter string.

4

Deliver with Sincerity

Copy the URL or scan the dynamic QR code and send it via text message, DM, or email. When the receiver opens it, they will unlock a responsive envelope with a heart-warming surprise.
